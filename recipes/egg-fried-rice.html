Egg Fried Rice

Fried rice is a dish of cooked rice that has been stir-fried in a wok or a frying pan and is usually mixed with other ingredients such as eggs, vegetables, seafood, or meat.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup water
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 cup uncooked instant rice
  • 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
  • ½ onion, finely chopped
  • ½ cup green beans
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper

Step by Step:

  1. In a saucepan bring water, salt and soy sauce to a boil. Add rice and stir. Remove from heat, cover and let stand 5 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a medium skillet or wok over medium heat. Saute onions and green beans for 2 to 3 minutes.
  3. Pour in egg and fry for 2 minutes, scrambling egg while it cooks.
  4. Stir in the cooked rice, mix well and sprinkle with pepper.
