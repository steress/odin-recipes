Bulgarian Tarator - Cold Cucumber Soup

This is an extremely popular summertime dish in Bulgaria. It serves as a replacement for salad or appetizers.

It tastes similar to tzatziki, so it's a very bright-flavored, cooling, and refreshing dish!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cucumbers, peeled and cut into small cubes
  • 1 (16 ounce) container plain yogurt
  • Â¼ cup minced fresh dill
  • 4 cloves garlic, pressed
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon water, or as needed (Optional)
  • 10 ice cubes, or as needed

Step by Step:

  1. Place cucumbers in a large bowl.
  2. Whisk yogurt in a separate bowl using a fork until yogurt is smooth and liquefied; pour over cucumbers.
  3. Add dill, garlic, oil, and salt to cucumber mixture; toss to coat.
  4. Stir in enough water to reach desired consistency. Refrigerate until chilled, at least 30 minutes.
  5. Ladle soup into serving bowls; add about 2 ice cubes to each bowl.
